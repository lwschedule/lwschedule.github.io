About LW Schedule

LW Schedule is a fast, installable class schedule app for students at Lynbrook High School. It shows today, this week, or this month in two taps; quietly notifies you before each period, and reminds you to drop your phone in the caddy or pack up before the bell.

Everything runs locally on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no data ever leaves your phone.
